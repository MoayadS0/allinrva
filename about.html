We Are All In!

And we love creating exciting and inclusive events that bring people together and build community! With our small but growing team of artists, musicians, chefs, designers, and more, we hope to deliver a party that will have you And your friends going all in to have an all out good time!

Our Story

Starting with a few small events during the pandemic to promote local beauty and wellness businesses, we've grown to a small army of employees and volunteers, working to promote local craftspeople and service providers, and to give back to the local communities and local scenes we love! Our business and our private events all go back to supporting that mission.

Our People

Proudly femme- and queer- owned and operated in Richmond, VA since 2020, our passion for diversity and inclusion is the reason why we do what we do! So we design our events around equity, accessibility, and inclusion, because we design parties our friends could come to. A party without exclusion. A party that's All In!

Leigh Anne Zylstra "Leg!"

Entrepreneur, influencer, and licensed social worker with a Master's from VCU, Leigh Anne has a broad skill set and diverse array of projects. But perhaps what defines her most is the deep dedication she applies to every passion project, whether it be running a group home, starting a gluten-free baking company, or planning her next concert adventure with a squad of friends.

Trouble Medas "Trubbs!"

Organizer and activist with 20 years of experience in human services, caring industries, and non-profit work. Trubbs' is a community builder, and their sense of vocation for Richmond and especially the local queer community drives their work. In addition to event planning they work as a Healthcare Coordinator for local mental health non-profit Childsavers. There are rumors they are currently recording a solo singer-songwriter project.
